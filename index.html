HOW TO PLAY

You are about to embark on an adventure through jungle, temple, cave, and beyond — but the path you take is up to you.

  • Each screen presents a question about my approach to the role.
  • Select the question you most want answered.
  • My answer will appear as the explorer sets off for the next location — read at your own pace.
  • Hit CONTINUE once you're ready to press on.
  • Reach the end to complete the adventure!
